High Concept

Have you ever felt like the road is filled with a million obstacles? With Nervewrecked, it is! It's much like GTA's driving, but from a top-down view.



Genre

Nervewrecked is a top-down endless runner in the style of a driving game.



Platform

Nervewrecked is a universal game that can be played on any device.



Story

You are driving along the street until all of a sudden, the road becomes a minefield of obstacles that you must weave your way through. Since this is an endless game, the only real player motivation is rooted in the want to constantly beat your personal high score.



Aesthetics

Graphic Style: pixelated.
Music: 8-bit loops.
Sounds: when obstacles are hit and the sound of the car driving.



Gameplay

Mechanics

The whole objective of the game is to avoid obstacles strewn across the road. There are many different types of obstacles, such as shopping carts and traffic cones. Avoiding one obstacle awards you one point. If the player hits three obstacles, the game ends. Otherwise, the game is endless. The goal is simply to get the highest score you can.

Controls

Keyboard: left and right arrow keys.
Touch: left and right side of screen.

Onboarding/Player Learning

The simple instructions and controls are presented to the user at the beginning of the game.
